UAJK AI Society

University of Azad Jammu & Kashmir

The UAJK Artificial Intelligence Society (UAJK AIS) represents a research and development guild dedicated to exploring applied machine learning fields. MedAI is a clinical screening suite leveraging scikit-learn classifiers trained on global epidemiological databases.

Core Academic Mandates

  • Bridging academic machine learning research with practical software architecture.
  • Ensuring clinical data pipeline alignment through standardization.
  • Empowering local student and research portfolios with production-quality prototypes.
